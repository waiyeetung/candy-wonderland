ABOUT US

The Candy Wonderland is a sweet and immersive experience that brings the history and culture of candy to life. From classic confections to modern treats, our exhibits showcase the evolution of candy-making and its influence around the world. Perfect for candy lovers of all ages, the museum offers a unique journey through the sights, smells, and stories of your favorite sweets. Come explore the world of sugar, creativity, and fun!
